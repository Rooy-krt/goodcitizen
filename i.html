We Aim At

Inspiring Responsible Citizens

The Good Citizen Campaign (GCC) is a citizen-led initiative focused on raising a new generation of responsible, value-driven citizens—starting with children, teens, and youth. It encourages young people to grow in character, care for their communities, and love their country. By teaching the importance of self-discipline, compassion, and patriotism early in life, we lay the foundation for a stronger society.

Being a good citizen starts with you. We encourage young people to grow morally, work hard, build good habits, and keep learning—so they can be their best every day.

Our Programs

To bring our mission to life, the Good Citizen Campaign runs through three key programs:

Organized conversations between children, teens, and youth with community leaders, national figures, and inspirational individuals from various fields. These forums provide mentorship, civic education, and real-life stories that motivate responsible action.

A dedicated space for younger participants to receive inspirational messages, engage in value-based activities, and connect with role models who help shape their character and ambitions.

A celebration of citizenship in action. This includes an online platform and live award ceremonies that document and recognize acts of civic responsibility, community service, leadership, and personal excellence among children and youth.

We are community-driven initiative

This program is aimed at promoting and encouraging positive behaviors, actions, and values among children mostly and members of the community at large. The campaign is focused on fostering civic engagement, social responsibility and ethical conduct.

Our Credo

I am a Good Citizen It is my responsibility – Ni jukumu langu Kutenda wema kwangu, (To better myself) Kutenda wema kwa wenzangu, (To better my neighbor) Kutenda wema kwa nchi yangu. (To better my country)

Our Core Values

Integrity – I do what is right, even when no one is watching. Respect – I treat everyone with kindness and fairness. Responsibility – I take ownership of my actions and do my part. Servant Leadership – I lead by helping others and setting a good example.
